# ESILV Engineering Programs (Sample Document)

> Synthetic demo document for the RAG pipeline. Not an official ESILV document.

ESILV is a French engineering school that awards a five-year *Diplôme d'Ingénieur*.
The curriculum is organised as a two-year integrated preparatory cycle followed by a
three-year engineering cycle.

In the engineering cycle, students choose one of four majors:

- **Data & Artificial Intelligence** — machine learning, deep learning, big data, and
  data engineering.
- **Computer Science & Digital Technologies** — software engineering, cybersecurity,
  and cloud computing.
- **Energy & Smart Cities** — sustainable energy systems and smart infrastructure.
- **Industrial Engineering & Numerical Mechanics** — modelling, simulation, and
  manufacturing.

All majors share a common core in mathematics, programming, project management, and
foreign languages. The Data & Artificial Intelligence major includes courses in Python,
statistics, natural language processing, and computer vision, and culminates in an
end-of-studies project carried out with an industrial partner.

The standard language of instruction is French, with several tracks fully available in
English. Students can also spend one or two semesters abroad through partner
universities.
