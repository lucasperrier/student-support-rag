ESILV Academic Calendar 2025-2026 (Sample Document)

Synthetic demo document for the RAG pipeline. Not an official ESILV document.

The academic year is divided into two semesters.

Autumn semester:
- Start of classes: early September 2025
- All Saints break: one week at the end of October 2025
- End-of-semester examinations: January 2026
- Winter break: two weeks from late December 2025 to early January 2026

Spring semester:
- Start of classes: late January 2026
- Spring break: two weeks in April 2026
- End-of-semester examinations: June 2026
- Summer break: from July 2026

Internships:
- Engineering-cycle students complete a final internship of four to six months,
  usually starting in the spring of the final year.
- The internship agreement (convention de stage) must be signed before the internship
  begins.

Examinations:
- Resit examinations are organised at the end of each semester for students who did not
  validate all their credits.
- Detailed dates are published on the student portal at the start of each semester.
