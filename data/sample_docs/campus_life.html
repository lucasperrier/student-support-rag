ESILV shares a campus with two partner schools in the Paris business district of La Défense. Students have access to study spaces, fabrication labs, a library, and sports facilities.

Student associations

Student life is organised around many associations and clubs covering sports, technology, arts, humanitarian work, and entrepreneurship. The Student Union (Bureau des Élèves, or BDE) coordinates events throughout the year and organises the annual integration weekend.

Every association must respect the school's association charter, which sets out the rights and duties of clubs, including financial transparency and the rules for organising events on campus.

Health and well-being

A student health service offers medical appointments and psychological support. The school has a zero-tolerance policy on harassment and provides a confidential reporting procedure for any incident of sexual or gender-based violence.

Student card

The student card also serves as a badge for access to the buildings and as a payment card for the campus cafeteria. A lost card must be reported so it can be deactivated and replaced.
