# Admissions (Sample Document)

> Synthetic demo document for the RAG pipeline. Not an official ESILV document.

There are two main entry points to ESILV.

**Post-baccalauréat admission (first year).** Candidates who hold or are preparing a
scientific or technological baccalauréat apply through the national Parcoursup
platform. Selection is based on the academic record, a motivation statement, and, for
shortlisted candidates, an interview. Applications usually open in January and close in
March.

**Parallel admission (later years).** Students who have already completed one or two
years of higher education (CPGE, university, or a two-year technical degree) may join
the engineering cycle directly. These candidates apply through a dedicated admissions
file and a subject test, followed by an interview.

International students can apply through the international admissions office. A proof of
English proficiency (such as TOEFL or IELTS) is required for the English-taught tracks,
and a French language certificate is recommended for the French-taught tracks.

Tuition fees are published each year on the official school website. Scholarships and
work-study (apprenticeship) contracts are available for eligible students.
