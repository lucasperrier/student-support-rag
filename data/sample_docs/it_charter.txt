ESILV IT Charter - Summary (Sample Document)

Synthetic demo document for the RAG pipeline. Not an official ESILV document.

This charter describes the acceptable use of the school's IT resources, including the
network, computers, software, and student accounts.

Accounts:
- Each student receives a personal account to access the Wi-Fi network, the student
  portal, and the learning management system.
- Account credentials are personal and must not be shared with anyone.

Acceptable use:
- IT resources are provided primarily for academic purposes.
- Users must not attempt to access data or systems they are not authorised to use.
- Downloading or sharing illegal content is strictly forbidden.

Equipment:
- Students are expected to bring a personal laptop that meets the recommended technical
  specifications published before the start of the year.
- The recommended configuration includes a recent processor, at least 16 GB of memory,
  and enough storage for development tools.

Security:
- Users must keep their operating system and antivirus up to date.
- Any security incident or lost device must be reported to the IT support desk.

Violations of the charter may lead to disciplinary measures as defined in the school's
internal regulations.
